Block Mining Simulator

Mine blocks by finding a nonce that produces a hash starting with "00". Then watch what happens when you tamper with Block 1 — that's immutability.

How it works: Click "Mine Block" to increment the nonce until SHA-256 hash of (data + previous hash + nonce) starts with 00. Block 2 links to Block 1's hash — change Block 1's data and Block 2 breaks automatically.
